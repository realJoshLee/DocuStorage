App Install

This will walk you through on how to install our app. Our apps are based off of a PWA so that there isn't recompiling apps for a self hosted instance. The PWA app is supported for iOS and Android.


iOS Install

To install the app on iOS, add the app to the home screen.

Click on the share button on the handle bar to the right of the search bar.



Scroll down in the panel that pops up and click on Add to Home Screen.



Click on the Add button to add the app to your deice. When you open the app it will work just like any other app.
