iOS Install - For Safari

To install the app on iOS, add the app to the home screen.

Click on the share button on the handle bar to the right of the search bar or if you're on an iPhone on the bottom of the screen.






Scroll down in the panel that pops up and click on Add to Home Screen.






Click on the Add button to add the app to your deice. When you open the app it will work just like any other app.
